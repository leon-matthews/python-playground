
====
Grub
====

Simple utility to track and motivate me on my weight loss.

I weight myself on a digital scale every day and make a quick note about how things are
going. This program provides a daily target and plots my progress.

This is my interpretation of the `The Hacker's Diet by John Walker
<http://www.fourmilab.ch/hackdiet/>`_.
